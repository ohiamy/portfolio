    The person behind the work

    AW

    Amy Wong
    Lead UX Designer

    I'm a Lead Product Designer with 10+ years of experience designing products that scale, across health tech, entertainment, HR, and enterprise software

    But honestly, it started way before that. In high school I was building websites in Notepad, creating graphics and giving them away for free, writing tutorials for other people learning to do the same. Design and the web just made sense to me.

    These days I'm drawn to the systems side of design: how things scale, how teams operate, and how good design thinking gets built into an organization's DNA. I currently lead UX at ADP, and I'm actively growing into the strategic, operational side of the craft.

    I've also freelanced, been laid off, and built my way back. That range of experience shapes how I work: with clarity, adaptability, and a lot of intentionality.

    Design philosophy

    Good design is invisible in the right moments and expressive in the right moments. It doesn't call attention to itself — it calls attention to what the user needs to do next. I'm not interested in design for its own sake. I'm interested in design that changes how people work, how organizations function, and occasionally how people feel about themselves in relation to technology.
